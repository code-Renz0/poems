Do you keep a diary?
I do, not the regular kind (like the ones with dates and stuff that talks about one's day)but a different kind.

I find myself reflecting, especially when alone.

They elude me cause they do not wish to be written, because if they were then they are nomore hidden but free to wrick untold havoc.

myself

Frendship!
An abstract noun, as volatile as it is unseen, a bond stronger than blood.
It's pull like gravity, grounding us to those we feel ourselves the most with.
We presume to choose but it chooses us.
Like a web it weaves,piecing us together like uneven pieces of a puzzle, our diversities not withstanding
Forged from human emotions love and kindness, pain and anger and more
It bends but can brake, strengthend and weakens, can turn to hate.
It's hard as steel, brittle as clay.

My mind!
A being of it's own,
in constant contrast with my every thought
an infinite pool of possibilities,
a void as endless as space and time itself.
It speaks to me, like a voice from deep within,
soft as the wind it courses through me with words as clear as visions.
like a soothsayer, a seer, an oracle,it tells me tales .
tales of dreams and nightmares, of joy and sadness, of pain and pleasure, of fantacies that may never be.
it speaks of hidden truths and fears, of struggles deep down, of lonliness, of shame.
it is a ward,a demension, a plane , a tatrous for the most vile of thoughts,
my very own asylum.
